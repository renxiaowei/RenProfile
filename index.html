Last month, I got my M.S. degree in the School of Information and Communication Engineering of Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications(BUPT). I really appreciate my advisor Prof. Yuan Dong for his guidance and help in the past three years. My research interests include Image Retrieval, Machine Learning, Object Recognition, Visual Reranking, Deep Learning. I received my B.S. degree in the same university in June 2011.

I am also an intern at France Telecom Orange Labs - Beijing since April 2011. This is a very exciting place. I will continue to work here until June 2014. Under the supervision of Dr. Hongliang Bai, I have always been working on the research projects "Sports Highlight Detection - Orange Sports".

News! My thesis "SEMI-SUPERVISED CONTENT-BASED IMAGE RETRIEVAL" is awarded "Best Master Degree Paper in BUPT"!
